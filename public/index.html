About Us

Achieve Your Personal Fitness Goals

Improve your health, fitness and physical performance with Active Habits. Learn more about your body and push your limits to see what you're capable of doing. With our small group classes we are able to provide a more individual approach to group style strength and conditioning training. Active Habits is here to help you achieve all your personal fitness goals.

Expert Training & Nutrition System

We specifically design customised nutrition plans tailored to your needs and body type. Our expert staff continually evaluate your performance and adjust your personalised nutrition program to break past any plateaus. Our training philosophy consist of strength and conditioning work which is proven to be the most beneficial to lose weight, tone up and build muscle!

Mindset & Knowledge

We are firm believers in that the way that you think, determines the results you'll achieve. We open your mind up to ensure that you can achieve whatever body you desire. We help you develop a mindset that will benefit you not only at training, but in everyday life. At Active Habits we continually educate our clients on all aspects related to training and nutrition.
