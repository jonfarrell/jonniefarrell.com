Our Service

Active Habits is a Group Fitness Service with an integrated approach to health and fitness through our core principles of Training, Nutrition and Education. At Active Habits we believe these three core principles go hand in hand with each other, ensuring that you achieve the results you deserve.

Our Training style differs from our competition as we use a strength and conditioning approach which has been proven to increase lean muscle mass, lose fat, and tone up. All our nutrition programs are 100% personalised tailored to suit your goals, needs and lifestyle, making it sustainable and helping you live a balanced life. We understand how frustrating it is exercising each day and eating healthy, and the results just wont show.
